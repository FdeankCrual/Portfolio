How It All Began

The seed thought behind WIGNET (Welocity Institute of Genomic Nutrition and Entrepreneurship Training) stemmed from a simple, yet heartbreaking observation. Despite incredible advancements in modern medicine, chronic lifestyle diseases—diabetes, hypertension, obesity—were rising at unprecedented rates in India.

We realized that the clinical approach was entirely reactive. People were being treated only after the symptoms appeared. True healthcare had to be predictive. It had to look at the very foundation of human life: our DNA.

"What if we could teach professionals to decode genetic susceptibilities before they become diseases? What if food could act as the ultimate medicine?"

Our Vision

Our vision is to aggressively create mass awareness amongst common people about the amazing lifecycle benefits of genomic wellness globally. We envision a society where predictive healthcare and precise nutritional input prevent catastrophic lifestyle illnesses entirely. We believe in a world where individuals take charge of their biology instead of becoming victims of generic medical statistics.

Our Mission

To provide rigorous, career-oriented academic education in the rapidly growing field of Nutrigenomics. Our primary mission is to create thousands of skillful, empathetic nutrigenomics professionals. Through the Welocity Wellness ecosystem, we aim to empower passionate educators and wellness entrepreneurs to spread the science of living simple, gene-optimized, and fundamentally happy lives.

Our Core Philosophy

"Go Back to Nature. Let Food be Thy Medicine. Live a simple, organic, and happy life."
